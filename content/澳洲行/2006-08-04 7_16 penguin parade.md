# 7/16 penguin parade

文章日期：2006-08-04 12:13
![[penguin parade.jpg]]
We visit kangaroos, horses, sheep, cows, swan, koala and other animals, after a long time bus traveling, we finally arrived Penguin parade, because it is forbidden to take photos and videos near penguins, so we just took some picture outside.
This tour is quite difference to the Great Ocean Road , the driver only talk to herself and didn’t introduce us much associated the scenery. The price didn’t include dinner, so I have to paid $6 for fried rice and $3.8 for a little bowl of wonton soup.
到Phillip Island看企鵝，其實有點空虛，因為主秀(企鵝返巢不能拍照)，所以就出現了合成照片的服務。
到達園區之後一下子就逛完遊客中心，所以也只能在海邊靜候小企鵝出現，冬天返巢的只有一些小企鵝，不過遊客也因此比較少，大多數人都可以清楚看到小企鵝。個人認為，這趟又貴又浪費時間的團還是不太划算，因為在現場看企鵝還不如看錄影帶上來得清楚呢！
