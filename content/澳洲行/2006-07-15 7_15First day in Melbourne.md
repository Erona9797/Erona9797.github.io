# First day in Melbourne

文章日期：2006-07-15 19:52
![[find a bargain.jpg]]
After 11 hours longtime waiting for transit airplane in Vetnam airport, we finally arrived Melbourne at the morning on July 13th.
We spent $24 for Airport to city skybus return ticket, after about half an hour we arrived the Elizabeth Hostel.
The hostel counter is chinese who come from Jian-Su, so I can asked him for lots of useful information. Victoria Queen market just at the corner. The first thing we have to do is find something to fill up our stomach. Everything in Australia are expensive than Taiwan. one piece of Pizza cost $2.5, one dumpling cost $0.5 cent, one bread cost $2 ......
Approximately twice than Taiwan. We find a real bargain in Queen markert, because it is cheaper, what I  mean cheaper is just similar price in Taiwan.
A piece of Pizza cost $2.5, a bread also cost $2.5, we decided to buy a pizza or sandwich, but when we pay $2.5, the seller(vender) give us two piece, because it time to close the shop. so, we bought a real bargain.
